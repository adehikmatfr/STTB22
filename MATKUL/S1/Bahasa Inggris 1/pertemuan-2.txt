PARESTUSITIPAPETI

TENSES -> penamaan > pola > penggunaan
TENSES -> any of the form of a verb witch show the time at witch an action happened (bentuk kata kerja yang menunjukan waktu terjadinya perubatan/peristiwa)

- PAST TENSE
i sang yesterday. (waktu lampau)
- PRESENT TENSE
1 sing evry day. (sekarang)
- FUTURE TENSE
i will sing tomorrow. (masa depan)

- tingkat kesempurnaan peristiwa atau perubatan
1. simple -> bersifat umum (general)
2. continuous -> bersifat khusus (spesific)
3. perfect -> penekanan hasil (result)
4. perfect continuous -> penekanan durasi (duration) 

A. penamaan TENSES
simple -> aspect + TENSE
continuous, perfect, perfect continuous -> TENSE + aspect
