so there are 3 important components on the internet.
the first is the computer or device
 - A computer is an electronic device designed to process, store, and retrieve data quickly and efficiently. 
the second is the world
 - The definition of world here, is that the scale or reach of the internet must cover the entire world.
the 3rd is the Computer networking
 - A computer network is a connection between two or more computers that allows them to communicate with each other and share resources.

In short, the internet is a bridge that connects computers and devices around the world.
